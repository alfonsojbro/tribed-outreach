# Mode 1 — Instagram cold DM

Apply the core principles at the casual end of the register: texting a friend you respect. Two to four sentences, under ~350 characters. Name the app when it makes the gift feel real ("a demo of a Recovery Athletes app"), optional in shorter skeletons. One or two capabilities woven into a sentence (AI coaching, tracking, a community space) is fine; three or more is a feature list, cut.

## Reading the screenshot

Pull before writing: first name or handle (first name warms it up), the one anchor detail (rule zero), their own vocabulary for their audience ("athletes", "warriors", "mi gente"), program or challenge names, language and region cues (Spanish bio, voseo, .ar links, location, flag emoji). If no anchor detail is visible, ask the user, don't pad.

## Examples

These show register and shape. No two share a skeleton, every one hangs on a detail that only fits that coach. Never lift phrases from them verbatim.

**Detail-first, English, recovery coach:**
that reel on sleep being worth more than any supplement got me, I've sent it to two people already. I build apps for coaches and your page was basically asking for one, so I made a demo of a Recovery Athletes app with your method in it. want me to send it?

**Confession, English, fasting coach:**
ok this is a little forward but your autophagy explainer finally made fasting click for me, and I ended up building a demo app around your method. fasting timer, AI coaching, a spot for your community, the whole thing. it's done and it's yours to look at, wanna see it?

**Reveal-first, River Plate Spanish, mindset coach:**
che, te hice una app. en serio, ya existe. me quedé pensando en tu post sobre hábitos que no dependen de la motivación y terminé armando una demo con tu método adentro, coaching con IA y seguimiento para tu gente. ¿te la paso?

**Idea-first, neutral Spanish, movement coach:**
hola! tu serie de movilidad de cadera está muy bien armada, se nota el método detrás. se me ocurrió que eso ya es prácticamente una app, así que hice una demo con seguimiento y guía para tus alumnos. ¿quieres verla?
