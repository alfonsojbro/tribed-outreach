# Mode 3 — Follow-up sequences (no reply to the opener)

Three touches after the opener, then stop. Each lighter than the last, each from a different angle. Never guilt, never "just following up", never reference how many times you've messaged. If the user doesn't say which touch, assume the first. (If the prospect opened the demo but went quiet, that's Mode 5 territory, read references/postdemo.md instead.)

**Touch 1, day 3-4, the light bump.** Shortest message of the sequence, one or two sentences. They're busy, the thing exists and is theirs, door open. No new pitch.

**Touch 2, day 10, a new angle.** Needs a fresh reason to land. Best: react to something they posted since, which makes the touch feel natural (strongest play on Instagram, works on LinkedIn too). If nothing new, reframe the gift from a different side: the thirty seconds it takes to look, or one new tiny detail ("added your logo to it btw"). One soft question.

**Touch 3, day 18-21, the soft close.** The takeaway: you'll leave their inbox alone, the demo is theirs whenever with no expiry, wish them well. Lightest possible CTA or none. This often pulls a reply precisely because it removes all pressure. Then stop, silence after a clean takeaway is an answer.

Stop immediately at any reply, any positive signal, or any no.

Channel register: Instagram casual, LinkedIn a notch more professional, never corporate. Vary the wording across prospects, follow-ups are short, which makes copy-paste sameness even easier to spot.

## Examples

**Touch 1, English, LinkedIn:**
no pressure at all James, just didn't want this buried. the demo's still sitting here whenever you want a look.

**Touch 2, Instagram, reacting to new content:**
that new reel on hip mobility was a clean breakdown. reminded me your demo's still here waiting on you, want the link?

**Touch 3, neutral Spanish:**
Hola Lucía, te dejo tranquila con esto. La demo queda tuya para cuando te dé curiosidad, no vence. Te deseo lo mejor con todo.

**Touch 3, River Plate:**
Che Martín, te dejo el buzón en paz. La app queda tuya para cuando quieras, no se vence. Lo mejor para vos.
